# Battery Domain Knowledge

## Overview
The Battery domain contains telemetry and diagnostics for the vehicle power system, including voltage, current, temperature, and charge state.

## Key Battery Signals
- `BATT_V`: Battery voltage
- `BATT_I`: Battery current
- `BATT_TEMP`: Battery temperature
- `BATT_SOC`: State of charge

## Use Cases
- Detect charge/discharge abnormalities
- Identify thermal events affecting battery health
- Validate battery measurements against safe operating ranges
